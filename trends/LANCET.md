## Editorial Focus & Journal Identity

The Lancet positions itself as the leading voice on global health policy, social determinants of health, and high-impact clinical trials. In 2026, its editorial agenda is shaped heavily by:
- **Non-communicable disease (NCD) burden** with a renewed push on liver disease
- **Vaccine science and implementation** — especially malaria and infectious disease control
- **Climate and health** — sustained activism framing
- **Research integrity** — AI-generated fabricated references emerging as a new threat
- **Conflict and health systems** — Lebanon, Sudan, Gaza coverage prominent
