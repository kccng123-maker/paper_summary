## Editorial Focus & Journal Identity

The BMJ is the leading UK-based general medical journal with a strong tradition of clinical practice, NHS policy, and investigative health journalism. In 2026, the BMJ's editorial agenda spans:
- **Vaccine safety and policy** — aluminium adjuvants, COVID vaccines, flu mRNA vaccines
- **Infectious disease outbreaks** — hantavirus (cruise ship), tuberculosis
- **NHS and UK health policy** — Reform UK impact, royal colleges, hospital-at-home
- **AI in medicine** — sceptical stance on AI diagnostic claims
- **Global conflict and health** — Lebanon, Gaza, Iran, Sudan
- **Women's and reproductive health** — abortion access, pregnancy complications, pelvic pain
